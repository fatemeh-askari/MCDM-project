Applications

Multi-Criteria Decision

prioritization strategies

It involves evaluating alternatives based on multiple criteria to make informed choices, balancing conflicting objectives to reach optimal decisions.

Fuzzy Methods

ranking fuzzy numbers

Fuzzy methods accommodate imprecise or uncertain information, providing a framework for decision-making in situations where outcomes are not entirely clear-cut.

Multi-Objective Decision

prioritization strategies

It addresses problems with multiple, often conflicting, objectives, aiming to find solutions that optimize across these diverse goals.

Data Envelopment Analysis

homogeneous decision-making units

DEA is a quantitative method used to assess the relative efficiency of decision-making units, such as companies or organizations, by comparing their inputs and outputs.
